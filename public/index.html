Documentation

A quick technical overview of the IdeaForge AI pipeline.

1

Submit an idea

Describe your startup in one paragraph. This is sent to POST /api/workflows/ideaValidationWorkflow/start-async.

2

Idea Intake Agent

Extracts industry, target users, and problem category as structured JSON — the foundation for every downstream agent.

3

Market Research & Competitor Intel

Each checks Qdrant for semantically similar past research before generating anything new — reducing cost and latency on repeat queries.

4

Validation Agent

Produces a weighted 0–100 score across four factors (problem severity, demand, feasibility, differentiation) — each with a stated reason, not a black-box number.

5

Strategy, MVP & Risk Agents

Generate a go-to-market plan, phased MVP roadmap, and categorized risk assessment based on all prior findings.

6

Founder Report + Enkrypt AI Gate

The final report is compiled, then scanned by Enkrypt AI for bias, toxicity, and policy violations before being returned to you.
